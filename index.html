Why Download Sandesh?

Sandesh was created with a simple mission — to make communication faster, safer, and truly ours. In today's world, most chat apps are filled with ads, data tracking, and distractions. We wanted to build something clean, secure, and designed with privacy in mind. Sandesh is made by students, for everyone — connecting people with trust and simplicity.
